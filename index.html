How We Work

Four decisions that determine whether you win or lose before a single word is written.

Every engagement follows the same structure. Here is what working with our team looks like from the moment you bring us a solicitation to the day you receive the award notice.

01

Opportunity Analysis

We review the full solicitation, evaluate fit against your capabilities, identify evaluation criteria and scoring weights, flag compliance risks, and give you a clear go or no-go recommendation before any writing begins. You never waste money bidding on contracts you cannot win.

Result: clarity before commitment
02

Strategy and Research

We map your strengths against the agency's stated priorities, research competing vendors, develop your win themes, and build the pricing strategy. This is where the proposal is won before a single word is written.

Result: a winning plan on paper
03

Proposal Development

We write all required volumes: technical approach, management plan, past performance, pricing narrative, and compliance matrix. Every section is written to evaluation criteria, not just to answer the question.

Result: a submission-ready proposal
04

Submission and Debrief

We conduct a final compliance review before submission. After award or rejection we provide debrief analysis to identify what worked and what to improve on the next bid.

Result: a contract or a lesson that pays for itself
