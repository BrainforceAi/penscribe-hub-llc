Services

From first registration to signed contract. Every step covered.

You do not need to understand government procurement to win government contracts. That is our job. Below is everything we do for clients from day one through award.

Core Services

Proposal Preparation

End-to-end proposal writing: technical volumes, pricing narratives, compliance matrices, past performance.

RFP and Solicitation Analysis

Detailed breakdown of RFPs and RFQs: risks, requirements, evaluation criteria, and a go or no-go recommendation.

SAM.gov Registration

Full setup and activation of your System for Award Management profile. Required for every federal contract.

SBA Certifications

Application support for 8(a), HUBZone, WOSB, EDWOSB, SDVOSB, and VOSB certifications.

Capability Statement

A one-page capability statement built for your target agencies, NAICS codes, and core competencies.

Additional Services

UEI, CAGE Code and DUNS

Obtaining your Unique Entity Identifier, CAGE Code, and DUNS number to establish federal vendor identity.

Contract Opportunity Research

Targeted research on federal, state, and local bid opportunities that match your business profile and capacity.

State and Local Vendor Registration

Registration on state procurement portals and local vendor databases for non-federal contracting.

DBE and MBE Certifications

Support for Disadvantaged Business Enterprise and Minority Business Enterprise certifications at state and local level.

Past Performance Documentation

Writing and formatting of past performance references and contract summaries for proposals and capability packages.

Subcontracting Plans

Development of small business subcontracting plans required for large prime contracts.

Sources Sought and RFI Responses

Drafting responses to Sources Sought notices and RFIs to position your firm ahead of formal solicitations.
