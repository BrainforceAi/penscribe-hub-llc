Contract 02, Municipal

Lot Cleaning Services for Code Enforcement

Seven Cleaning Service, LLC, City of Winter Park, Florida

Scope of Work

Comprehensive lot cleaning services for the City of Winter Park's Code Enforcement division: grass mowing for residential, commercial, and vacant lots; trimming and edging; full debris and litter removal; herbicide application for weed control; and grading and removal of accumulated dirt.

The contract required photographic documentation, compliance reporting, and safe chemical handling protocols.

Our Contribution

Our team developed the complete proposal response: cover letter, company history, scope of work response, technical specifications, performance metrics, equipment list, and full fee proposal table across four service categories.

We advised on positioning the client's SBA WOSB, EDWOSB, MBE, and WBE certifications to maximize their evaluation standing.

Contract Awarded

Seven Cleaning Service, LLC was awarded the lot cleaning contract by the City of Winter Park, Florida. The estimated annual contract value was $138,540. This was the client's entry into municipal government contracting.
