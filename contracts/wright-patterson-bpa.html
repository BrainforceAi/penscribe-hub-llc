Contract 04, Federal

Laundry Services Blanket Purchase Agreement

Tebebi LLC, 711th Human Performance Wing, Wright-Patterson AFB, OH

Scope of Work

Weekly laundry services for the 711th Human Performance Wing at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, Ohio. Approximately 235 pounds of weekly laundry volume across three service categories: Operational Equipment (OE), Facility Equipment (FE), and Residential Housing (RH).

Services included standard laundry processing, 160-degree Fahrenheit thermal treatment for contaminated linens per CDC healthcare standards, separate facility handling for infectious disease items, vehicle sanitation using EPA-approved germicidal agents, minor garment repairs at no additional cost, and 24-hour emergency delivery capability.

Our Contribution

Our team prepared the complete proposal package for this BPA: cover letter, executive summary, technical capability statement, and representations and certifications.

The technical capability statement was structured to address each of the five mandatory acceptability criteria in the Performance Work Statement: 160-degree wash capability, separate infectious disease facility, vehicle sanitation compliance, minor repair capability, and 24-hour emergency delivery. We wrote the compliance narrative to directly mirror the PWS requirements section by section, leaving no evaluation gap for the contracting officer.

We also structured the pricing narrative to reflect the three-category service segmentation required by the solicitation and positioned Tebebi LLC as technically compliant and operationally ready for the April 1, 2026 performance start.

Contract Awarded

Tebebi LLC was awarded the Laundry Services BPA for the 711th Human Performance Wing at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base. This is the second Air Force base laundry contract won by Tebebi LLC with proposals our team prepared, demonstrating repeatable results across different installations and contracting offices.
