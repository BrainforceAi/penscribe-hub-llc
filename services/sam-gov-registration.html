SAM.gov Registration

Full setup and activation of your System for Award Management profile. Required for every federal contract.

You cannot accept a single federal dollar without an active SAM.gov registration. Our team handles the full process from entity validation through activation: UEI, CAGE Code, NAICS selection, representations and certifications, and the small business profile setup that controls how contracting officers find you.

Deliverables

  • Entity validation and UEI assignment
  • CAGE Code application
  • NAICS code selection
  • Representations and certifications
  • Small business profile setup
  • Annual renewal scheduling

Timeline

2 to 4 weeks end-to-end. Most of the time is government processing, not work on your end.
