Proposal Preparation

End-to-end proposal writing: technical volumes, pricing narratives, compliance matrices, past performance.

This is the core service. When you bring us a solicitation, we write the entire proposal response, every required volume mapped section-by-section to the evaluation criteria. Not just answering the question, but writing to how the proposal will be scored.

Deliverables

  • Technical approach volume
  • Management plan
  • Past performance documentation
  • Pricing narrative and schedule
  • Compliance matrix
  • All representations and certifications
  • Final pre-submission compliance review
  • Submission support

Timeline

Typical engagement runs 2 to 6 weeks depending on solicitation complexity. We work to your submission deadline, not ours.

How to start

Send us a short note describing your business and your situation. If there is a specific solicitation on the table, include it. We will respond within one business day with next steps. If the first conversation is enough, there is no charge.
