Capability Statement

A one-page capability statement built for your target agencies, NAICS codes, and core competencies.

A capability statement is what a contracting officer looks at for fifteen seconds before deciding whether to call you. Get this wrong and nothing else matters. Our team writes a one-pager that surfaces your differentiators, certifications, codes, and contact in the order a CO scans them.

Deliverables

  • Core competencies narrative
  • Differentiator block
  • NAICS and PSC code listing
  • Certifications and set-asides
  • Past performance summary
  • Designed one-page PDF

Timeline

1 to 2 weeks from kickoff to final PDF.
